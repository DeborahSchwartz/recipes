Ingredients:
 - 2 cups sugar
 - 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
 - 3/4 cup cocoa
 - 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
 - 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 1 c warm water + 2 tablespoons flaxseed, soaked for 15 minutes
 - 1 cup non-dairy milk
 - 1/2 cup applesauce
 - 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
 - 1 cup boiling water

Directions:
 1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking
    pans.

 2. Mix the flaxseed into the warm water and let soak while doing other steps.
 
 2. In a large bowl, combine the sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda
    and salt.  Add flaxseed mixture, non-dairy milk, applesauce and vanilla. Mix until smooth.  Stir in boiling water (batter will be thin). Pour batter into prepared pans.
 
 3. Bake 30 to 35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out
    clean. Cool completely.
